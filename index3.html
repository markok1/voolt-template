FAQs

What if I already have a website?

Even if you have a website, Voolt Marketing can still help you. Just supply your current site and answer a few questions. We’ll set up your ad campaigns on Google and Facebook (Meta) automatically.

How does your pricing work?

With Voolt Marketing, you set your own advertising budget. Choose your budget from different options, turn auto-renewal on or off, and adjust it anytime. We handle the campaign setup, and each time someone clicks on your Google or Meta ad, the cost is deducted from your Voolt account balance. If your balance gets low, we’ll notify you, or if you have auto-renewal on, we’ll top it up for you.

Do I need to perform any manual tasks if I don't want to?

No, you don't! Voolt Marketing automates everything for you. Just provide us with some basic information about your business and location, and our AI handles the rest. Your ads on Google and Facebook (Meta) will be created, optimized, and managed automatically, so you can focus on your business.

How will my ads appear?

Once you set your budget, we find the best spots on Facebook (Meta) and at the top of Google search results, in the sponsored section, to show your ads to local people looking for services like yours.

How will I receive my new leads?

When someone clicks on your ad and fills out a form on your website, you’ll get the lead instantly in your Voolt account, and will be notified via email and SMS. If they call you, the call will be recorded and available in your dashboard.

Can I insert old customers on my leads page manually and manage all of them together?

Of course! You can add their information directly to your dashboard and manage them all in one place.

How does call recording with clients work?

Whenever a client calls you, Voolt will record the call and save it for you. This way, you can keep important information or listen to messages if you missed the call. We’ll also save their number so you can call them back or send SMS easily.

Can I track website visitors and traffic?

Yes! Voolt’s built-in tools show you how many people visit your site, what pages they view, and what content is popular. This helps you see how well your website is doing and make smart decisions to improve it.

Do you offer 24/7 Customer Service?

Yes! Our friendly support team is here to help anytime you need it. Whether it’s a technical question or help with customizing your site, you can reach us via live chat or email, day or night.
